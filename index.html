GoatDraw Goes International

South Korean Artist and Illustrator @GoatDraw wanted to create Branded Merchandise that tailored to their Fan's interest in their work and sell them internationally. Unfortunately, a large majority of their fanbase is within the U.S., and they only had experience selling at small conventions, through in-person sales. Working with them, I prototyped designs for branded merchandise, eventually settling on a large desk mat. With his approval, I procured a manufacturer and prepared an online storefront. After initial pre-sales I coordinated international shipping and distribution to their many happy fans!

D-Floe's Designs

Austin based Designer @D_Floe wanted to capture a variety of interests of his fans within a single collection and was interested in selling numerous specific items of a variety of characters and designs. Despite having sold through a digital storefront before, 'D-Floe' was unsure how to group and brand the items together to entice fans to purchase a bundle instead of a single item. First examining his lineup of characters and designs, I worked to identify popularity metrics using data from his social media, and coordinate a product spread that placed the most in-demand characters in the most expensive items.
